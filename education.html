Education Initiatives

Empowering Rohingya students through educational opportunities and resources

Our Educational Mission

At BRSU, we believe that education is the most powerful tool for empowerment and change. Despite the numerous barriers Rohingya students face in accessing quality education, we are committed to creating pathways to learning and academic excellence for our community.

Our educational initiatives focus on providing resources, mentorship, scholarship information, and advocacy to ensure that Rohingya students can pursue their educational dreams. We work to bridge gaps in formal education while creating informal learning opportunities that nurture critical thinking, leadership, and cultural identity.

Through partnerships with educational institutions, NGOs, and community organizations, we strive to expand educational access and create sustainable learning pathways for Rohingya youth at all levelsâ€”from primary education to university and beyond.

Our Educational Programs

Tutoring Support

Our volunteer tutors provide one-on-one and small group tutoring in core subjects including mathematics, science, languages, and social studies. Sessions are available both in-person at community centers and online to reach students in different locations.

Digital Literacy

Our digital literacy program equips students with essential computer skills, internet research capabilities, and digital communication tools. We provide access to computers, basic software training, and guidance on using technology for educational advancement.

Language Programs

We offer English language courses at various proficiency levels to help students develop strong communication skills. Additionally, we provide Burmese, Bangla, and Rohingya mother-tongue literacy programs to preserve our cultural identity and support multilingual education.

Mentorship Program

Our mentorship program connects Rohingya students with successful professionals and academics who provide guidance, support, and inspiration. Mentors help students navigate educational pathways, develop career goals, and build networks that support their academic journey.

Vocational Training

We partner with technical institutes to provide vocational training in areas such as computer skills, tailoring, crafts, and other marketable skills. These programs are designed to create alternative educational pathways and economic opportunities for Rohingya youth.

Scholarship Opportunities

BRSU is committed to helping Rohingya students access scholarship opportunities around the world. We provide information, application support, and preparation assistance for various scholarship programs.

Current Scholarship Resources:

  • DAFI Scholarship Program for refugee students
  • UNHCR Higher Education Scholarship Programme
  • Asian University for Women Scholarships
  • Mastercard Foundation Scholars Program
  • HOPES (Higher and Further Education Opportunities and Perspectives for Syrians)
  • Local university partnerships and tuition assistance programs

Scholarship Application Support

Our team provides guidance on scholarship applications, including essay writing, interview preparation, and document collection. Contact our education coordinator for more information on upcoming scholarship deadlines and application workshops.
