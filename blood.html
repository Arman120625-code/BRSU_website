Our Blood Donation Initiative

The BRSU Blood Donation Initiative was established in 2021 to address the critical shortage of blood supply in refugee camps and surrounding communities. Our program connects volunteer donors with patients in need, providing a lifeline during medical emergencies.

Through our network of student volunteers and partnerships with local hospitals and clinics, we organize regular blood donation drives, maintain a database of donors, and coordinate emergency blood requests. Our initiative has helped save hundreds of lives by ensuring timely access to safe blood for patients in critical condition.

We believe that blood donation is not just a medical necessity but also a powerful act of community solidarity. By donating blood, our members demonstrate their commitment to supporting one another and building a stronger, more resilient community.

Why Donate Blood?

Blood Donation Image

Save Lives

A single blood donation can save up to three lives. In emergency situations, during surgeries, or for patients with conditions like thalassemia, your blood donation can be the difference between life and death.

  • Help accident victims with severe blood loss
  • Support patients undergoing major surgeries
  • Assist cancer patients receiving chemotherapy
  • Aid women with complications during childbirth
Health Check Image

Health Benefits for Donors

Donating blood not only helps others but also offers several health benefits for donors. Each donation includes a mini health check-up and can help reduce the risk of certain health conditions.

  • Free health screening (blood pressure, hemoglobin, etc.)
  • Reduced risk of heart disease
  • Stimulates blood cell production
  • Burns calories and helps maintain iron levels

Community Impact

Beyond the immediate medical benefits, our blood donation initiative strengthens community bonds and promotes a culture of mutual support and solidarity. By donating blood, you become part of a vital network that ensures our community can respond effectively to medical emergencies.

"When my sister needed an emergency blood transfusion after childbirth, BRSU's blood donor network helped us find a matching donor within minutes. This initiative doesn't just save lives—it gives our community hope and security during our most vulnerable moments." — Fatima, community member

Become a Blood Donor

Join Our Donor Network

By registering as a blood donor with BRSU, you become part of our emergency response network. We'll contact you when your blood type is needed or when donation drives are organized in your area.

Our donor database is secure and confidential. Your information will only be used for blood donation purposes and will never be shared with third parties without your consent.

Benefits of Registration:

  • Regular health updates and screening
  • Priority access to blood products for you and your family
  • SMS alerts about upcoming donation drives
  • Recognition in our donor appreciation events
  • Donor ID card with blood type information

Emergency Blood Request

Need Blood Urgently?

In case of medical emergencies requiring blood transfusion, our emergency response team works quickly to identify and contact suitable donors from our database.

Emergency Contact:

24/7 Blood Helpline: +880 1234-567890

WhatsApp: +880 1234-567890

For non-urgent inquiries or scheduled surgeries requiring blood, please fill out the request form at least 3-5 days in advance to ensure we have adequate time to arrange donors.

What to Provide:

  • Patient's full name and age
  • Blood type needed (if known)
  • Number of units required
  • Hospital/clinic name and location
  • Doctor's name and contact (if available)
  • Nature of emergency/reason for transfusion
  • Contact person's name and phone number

A Mother's Life Saved

When Amina experienced severe postpartum hemorrhage after giving birth, she urgently needed blood transfusions. Through our emergency response system, three donors were quickly mobilized, and their donations helped save both Amina and her newborn daughter.

Emergency Surgery Success

When 12-year-old Kamal needed emergency surgery after an accident, his rare blood type made finding donors challenging. BRSU's database quickly identified matching donors, and within hours, the necessary units were collected, allowing doctors to perform the life-saving procedure.

"The speed at which BRSU found donors for my son was incredible. Their organization saved precious time when every minute counted." — Kamal's father
